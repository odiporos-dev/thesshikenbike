Thessaloniki Hike&Bike Privacy Policy

Information Collected

The application records your location to show your current position on the map, record a track and follow an existing track. All the tracklog are kept private to the application and may only be shared if the user chooses so (e.g. using the share activity). In case the user chooses the "Fused location client option" the app uses third party services that may collect information used to identify you. You may find privacy related information for this service in this link. No other information is implicitly collected or shared by the application.
